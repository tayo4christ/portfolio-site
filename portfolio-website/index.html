About Me

My introduction

My work spans advanced research in gesture recognition and assistive technologies, including the development of an AI-driven Makaton-to-English translation system presented at the ICSIE 2024 conference and published in the ACM Digital Library. I have also been featured in Punch Nigeria for my innovations in AI speech solutions. I am currently conducting research on detecting Apraxia of Speech using adversarial noise analysis and multimodal data, exploring how AI models respond to perturbed audio inputs to improve robustness and inclusivity in speech-based assistive technologies. I am a Professional Member of the British Computer Society (BCS), an Associate Member of IEEE, and a Fellow of the Royal Society of Arts (RSA).
